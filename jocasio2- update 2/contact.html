What to Include in Your Message

To get a useful response, the more context you can give, the better. The fields above ask for the basics, but in the message itself it helps to include:

  • How long you have been training and what your current routine looks like
  • Specific goals (hypertrophy, strength, body recomposition, sport-specific)
  • Constraints — how many days a week you can train, equipment access, injuries
  • What you have tried already and what is or is not working

The more detail, the more specific the response can be. Vague questions get vague answers, and there is no shortcut around that.
